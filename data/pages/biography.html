Biography

Born in Kollam, Kerala and brought up in Trichy, Tamilnadu, Vibin started his music from his early school days. He gradually played for bands and shows during schools days and culturals and was awarded too. His interest towards music and his supporting parents made him pursue music as his carrier. After his high school graduation he went on with his Sound Engineering.


Being a composer, Vibin has written for many short films & ads and was rewarded "The Best Background Score" for the film "Pidaaran" by a Television Channel named “Kailaingar TV”. His works attract many new film makers and is teaming up for their future projects. His tireless efforts of making good music will carry on.
